About d-Briefs

d-Briefs is your post-research destination — where briefs go after they've been crafted in Casebriefr. This gallery serves as:

  • Quick Reference: Instantly recognize and retrieve past case citations
  • Pattern Library: Build familiarity with frequently cited cases and legal principles
  • Portfolio: Showcase selected briefs for study, review, or collaboration
  • Archive: Save and organize completed research for future reference

Future features: Save partially completed briefs from Casebriefr, advanced search by jurisdiction or legal topic, and export options for your personal legal research database.
